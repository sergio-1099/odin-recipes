Lasagna

Description

The perfect lasagna made with parmesan cheese filling, melted mozarella, and robust tomato meat sauce make this the best lasagna recipe! You will be wanting to cook this everyday after trying it. There has always been something special about homemade lasagna that never grows old. Don't worry if you have no idea how to make this, we will walk you through it. Step-by-step.

This recipe can be frozen! This makes it perfect to make ahead of time, or for left-overs. You WON'T be able to let this go to waste.

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Tomato Meat Sauce
  • Lasagna Pasta

Steps

  1. Start by making a homemade meat sauce.
  2. Make a parmesan ricotta cheese mixture.
  3. Use your favorite lasagna noodles.
  4. Bring on the cheese!
  5. Bake covered for 15 minutes
