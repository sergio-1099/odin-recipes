Skillet Chicken

Description

This delicious dinner involves a skillet chicken in a sundried tomato parmesan cream sauce with fresh basil. It's a quick and easy dinner that will definitely make everyone love you!

This involves chicken breasts that are suteed in olive oil and seasoned with salt and pepper. It's tomato cream sauce is made with fresh garlic, thyme, red pepper flakes, heavy cream, parmesan cheese, sundried tomaoes and fresh basil. How long, you might ask? 30-minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chicken Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Sundried Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Start by heating olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Remove the chicken from the skillet and add finely diced garlic.
  3. Remove from heat and add chicken to the skillet.
  4. Top with fresh basil and freshly grated parmesan cheese.
